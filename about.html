OUR STORY & MISSION

Welcome to'Egypt’s Forgotten Treasures,'a project born from a passion to explore the untold stories of our ancient civilization. While iconic sites like the Pyramids are world-famous,thousands of other magnificent temples, villages, and monuments lie hidden across Egypt, waiting to be rediscovered.

We are dedicated to bringing these overlooked gems to the light. By documenting sites like the stunning Philae Temple (pictured), we aim to provide a fresh perspective on Egypt's heritage, fostering a deeper cultural appreciation and inspiring sustainable tourism to these incredible "forgotten" locations.
